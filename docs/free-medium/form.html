Free Medium

I must admit that I am using a feature that Medium offers: Reading totally for free coming from Twitter.

It means that if you see an article on Twitter, you could simply click on it and finished, it’s free now. The non-programmer mind would think “Let’s tweet it and use it!”

But the programmer mind from the middle east would say “Let’s make it a service and put ads on it!” and I am middle-eastern and I built the service FreeMedium.info where the process is made automatic and possible for free.

No ads so far and I post articles on it on regular basis, so it would make me more than happy if you check it out.
